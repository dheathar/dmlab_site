Research Areas

Exploring the frontiers of data science, artificial intelligence, and computational engineering

Web Engineering & Data Engineering

Our work in web and data engineering focuses on building scalable, robust systems for the modern digital ecosystem. We develop cutting-edge solutions for distributed data processing, API design, and full-stack web applications that serve millions of users.

Our research addresses challenges in data pipeline optimization, real-time data processing, and microservices architecture, with applications spanning e-commerce, healthcare, and government services.

Key Research Topics:

  • Distributed systems architecture
  • RESTful and GraphQL API design
  • Data pipeline optimization
  • Cloud-native application development
  • Microservices and containerization
  • Real-time data streaming platforms

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

We advance the state of the art in machine learning through both theoretical research and practical applications. Our work spans deep learning, reinforcement learning, and natural language processing, with a focus on developing AI systems that are robust, interpretable, and ethical.

Current projects include automated algorithm optimization, computer vision for medical imaging, and NLP systems for multilingual content analysis.

Key Research Topics:

  • Deep neural network architectures
  • Natural language processing (NLP)
  • Computer vision systems
  • Reinforcement learning
  • Explainable AI (XAI)
  • Transfer learning and few-shot learning

Audio Processing & Language Intelligence

Our research combines advanced audio signal processing with natural language processing to create intelligent systems for knowledge extraction, audio content understanding, and structured information generation. We leverage large language models for text analysis, retrieval-augmented generation, and automated data structuring from unstructured sources.

This work spans music information retrieval using machine learning, voice analysis, and embedded IoT systems with emphasis on intelligent audio applications. Applications include knowledge management platforms, smart audio devices, and automated content analysis systems.

Key Research Topics:

  • Natural language applications for text analysis and knowledge extraction
  • Large language models for structured-data generation from unstructured input
  • Advanced retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)
  • Information extraction and management techniques
  • Audio signal processing and voice analysis
  • Music information retrieval using machine learning
  • Embedded systems and IoT for intelligent audio applications
  • Speech recognition and synthesis
  • Audio feature extraction and pattern recognition
  • Real-time audio processing on edge devices

Big Data Analytics

In an era of exponentially growing data, we develop methodologies and tools for extracting meaningful insights from massive datasets. Our research combines statistical methods, machine learning, and domain expertise to solve real-world problems in healthcare, social protection, and environmental monitoring.

We specialize in handling structured and unstructured data at scale, employing modern frameworks for distributed computing and advanced visualization techniques.

Key Research Topics:

  • Large-scale data processing (Hadoop, Spark)
  • Predictive analytics and forecasting
  • Social network analysis
  • Time series analysis
  • Data visualization and dashboards
  • Anomaly detection systems

Parallel Processing & Computational Optimization

We investigate methods to accelerate computation through parallel algorithm design, GPU programming, and optimization techniques. Our research enables faster training of machine learning models, real-time processing of sensor data, and efficient simulation of complex systems.

Projects include developing novel parallel algorithms for graph processing, optimizing deep learning inference on edge devices, and creating high-performance computing solutions for scientific applications.

Key Research Topics:

  • Parallel algorithm design
  • GPU acceleration (CUDA, OpenCL)
  • High-performance computing (HPC)
  • Algorithm optimization techniques
  • Distributed computing frameworks
  • Energy-efficient computation

Digital Ecosystems

Our digital ecosystems research creates integrated platforms that connect stakeholders across education, labor markets, and social services. We design systems that facilitate collaboration, knowledge sharing, and efficient resource allocation in complex socio-technical environments.

Key applications include the AGORA platform for labor market services, digital education platforms, and social protection information systems.

Key Research Topics:

  • Platform economy and digital markets
  • Labor market information systems
  • Educational technology platforms
  • Social protection databases
  • Stakeholder collaboration tools
  • Digital skills assessment systems

Bioinformatics

At the intersection of biology and computer science, our bioinformatics research develops computational tools for analyzing biological data. We work with genomic sequences, protein structures, and medical imaging data to advance personalized medicine, drug discovery, and disease diagnosis.

Our interdisciplinary team collaborates with medical institutions and research centers to translate computational insights into clinical applications.

Key Research Topics:

  • Genomic sequence analysis
  • Protein structure prediction
  • Medical image processing
  • Computational drug discovery
  • Biological network analysis
  • Personalized medicine algorithms
