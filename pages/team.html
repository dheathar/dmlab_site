Core Research Team

Laboratory directors and principal investigators

Giannis Tzimas

Professor & Lab Director

Professor in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Peloponnese. His research activity spans Net-centric and Service-Oriented Information Systems, Web Engineering, Web Data Engineering, Big Data, Data Science, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Digital Ecosystems and Digital Transformation for the Public Sector, and Bioinformatics. He graduated from the Department of Computer Engineering and Informatics at the University of Patras.

Google Scholar Profile

Panagiotis Zervas

Associate Professor

Associate Professor in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Peloponnese since June 2020, specializing in Educational Technology, Audio Information Systems, and data-driven learning environments. His research focuses on developing innovative e-learning platforms, personalization systems, and applying data science methodologies to educational contexts.
