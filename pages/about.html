About DM Lab

Advancing research in data science, artificial intelligence, and digital innovation

Our Mission

The Data & Media Laboratory (DM Lab) was established in March 2020 under the Department of Informatics and Telecommunications at the University of Peloponnese. Our mission encompasses three core objectives:

  • Conduct high-level theoretical and applied research in data science and artificial intelligence
  • Support undergraduate and postgraduate studies with hands-on research experience
  • Foster collaboration with researchers, academic institutions, industry, and government organizations

"Interdisciplinarity is key to innovation"

We remain open to new collaborative challenges and partnerships that advance the frontiers of data science, pushing the boundaries of what's possible with intelligent systems and data-driven insights.

Research Excellence

Interdisciplinary Approach

Our research spans six core areas: Web Engineering & Data Engineering, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Big Data Analytics, Parallel Processing & Computational Optimization, Digital Ecosystems, and Bioinformatics. This interdisciplinary approach enables us to tackle complex, real-world problems from multiple perspectives.

Active Research Initiatives

The lab maintains four major ongoing research projects: Project 19-20 (COVID-19 data analytics tracking disease spread and societal effects), AGORA (talent-scouting mechanism creating digital ecosystem services for the labor market), For4Opt (automated algorithm selection and parameter tuning using machine learning), and Vocational Training (information management systems for vocational education).

Academic Integration

The lab serves as a training ground for undergraduate and graduate students, providing hands-on experience with state-of-the-art technologies and methodologies. We mentor PhD candidates, support thesis research, and offer internship opportunities to aspiring data scientists and engineers.

Impact & Collaboration

Industry Partnerships

We collaborate with leading technology companies and startups to translate research into practical applications.

Government Consulting

Our expertise informs policy decisions in areas such as labor markets, education, and digital transformation.

Academic Network

We maintain strong ties with research institutions worldwide, fostering knowledge exchange and joint initiatives.

Facilities & Resources

The DM Lab is equipped with modern computational infrastructure, including high-performance computing clusters, GPU servers for deep learning, and distributed data processing systems. Our researchers have access to extensive datasets, software licenses, and cloud computing resources to support cutting-edge investigations.

Located within the University of Peloponnese campus, the lab provides a collaborative workspace designed to facilitate innovation, with dedicated areas for team meetings, seminars, and student projects.

Join Us

Whether you're a prospective student, researcher, or industry partner, we welcome collaborations that advance the frontiers of data science and AI. Get in touch to explore opportunities.
