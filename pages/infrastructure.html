Access for Researchers

The DM Lab infrastructure is available to faculty members, research fellows, PhD students, and collaborators working on approved research projects. Access is granted based on project requirements and resource availability.

We provide training sessions on HPC systems, GPU programming, and distributed computing frameworks for new users. Our technical staff offers support for job scheduling, environment setup, and performance optimization.
