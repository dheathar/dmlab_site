ABOUT US

The Data & Media Lab

The Data & Media Laboratory (DM Lab) was founded in March 2020 and operates under the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering of the University of Peloponnese. DM Lab aims, among others, to:

Conduct high-level theoretical and applied research

Support the undergraduate and postgraduate studies of the department

Collaborate with researchers, research centers, and academic institutions

Collaborating with leading institutions

Research Excellence

Driving innovation across six core research domains

Web & Data Engineering

Building scalable web applications and robust data pipelines for modern digital ecosystems

  • Distributed systems architecture
  • API design and integration
  • Data pipeline optimization

AI & Machine Learning

Developing intelligent systems and machine learning applications for real-world challenges

  • Deep learning models
  • Natural language processing
  • Computer vision systems

Big Data Analytics

Extracting insights from massive datasets using advanced analytical techniques

  • Large-scale data processing
  • Predictive analytics
  • Real-time data streaming

Computational Optimization

Accelerating computation through parallel processing and algorithm optimization

  • Parallel algorithm design
  • GPU acceleration
  • Performance optimization
